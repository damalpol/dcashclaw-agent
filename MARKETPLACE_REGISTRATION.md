## 🌐 Marketplaces to Register

### 1. Fiverr (fiverr.com)
**Steps:**
1. Go to fiverr.com → Click "Join"
2. Sign up with email or Google account
3. Click "Become a Seller" → "Create a Gig"
4. Fill in:
   - Gig Title (use ideas above)
   - Category: Digital Marketing > SEO / Lead Generation / Social Media
   - Pricing: Set your 3 packages
   - Description: Use templates above
   - Requirements: Ask for website URL, ICP details, or brand guidelines
   - Gallery: Upload sample reports (create mockups if needed)
5. Publish gig

**Tips:**
- Start with lower prices to get first reviews
- Offer 24-hour delivery for first 10 orders
- Use keywords in title and description
