**Issue:** Viewport meta tag missing on 3 pages  
**Impact:** Poor mobile experience, lower mobile rankings

### 2. Slow Page Load Time
**Issue:** Average load time 4.2 seconds (target: <3s)  
**Impact:** High bounce rate, lower rankings  
**Fix:** 
- Enable image compression (WebP format)
- Minify CSS/JS files
- Implement lazy loading for images

### 3. Missing Meta Descriptions
**Issue:** 2 of 5 pages lack meta descriptions  
**Impact:** Lower click-through rates from search results  
**Fix:** Add unique meta descriptions (150-160 characters) to all pages
